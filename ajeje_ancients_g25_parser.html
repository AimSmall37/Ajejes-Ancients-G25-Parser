Ajeje's Ancients — G25 Sample Collection Parser

Loads raw Global25 rows (Name, PC1–PC25), splits the encoded name into Population, Sample, Age and Coverage fields, sorts every individual into an archaeological stratum by date, and can average coordinates by population.

Ajeje's Ancients is a Global25 sample collection created and maintained for years by Ajeje Brazorf, now maintained by ilabv. Global25 (G25) coordinates created by Davidski — background on the Eurogenes blog. This parser and the original R scripts it's based on were written by AimSmall.
